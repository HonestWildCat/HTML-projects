Cherry jam

Description:

Delicious pitted cherry jam is well suited for making desserts and on its own.

Ingridients:

  • 1 kg - cherries
  • 900 g - sugar

Cooking:

  1. To make cherry jam, measure the required amount of cherries and prepare sugar.
  2. Remove all twigs and leaves. Remove pits from cherries.
  3. Put the berries in a bowl. Sprinkle cherries with sugar. Cover and leave to stand for a few hours to allow the cherries to release their juice.
  4. When the berries start juice, put the pan on the fire, bring to a boil and boil them for 2-3 minutes. Then remove from heat, cover and leave to cool.
  5. So boil the jam 2 more times, allowing it to cool completely in between.
  6. Pour the boiling jam into the prepared jars. Close with lids. Jars of jam, after cooling, store in a cool place.
