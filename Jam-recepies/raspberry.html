Raspberry jam

Description:

The finished jam has a dark, rich raspberry color, deep taste and aroma. Similar in texture to jelly, but with whole berries.

Ingridients:

  • 1 kg - raspberries
  • 700 g - sugar

Cooking:

  1. Clean the collected berries from leaves and twigs.
  2. Be sure to rinse the berry with running cool water in the shower or dissecting the water with your hand. Let the water drain.
  3. Pour the washed raspberries immediately with 1/3 of the sugar, mix gently and leave until the juice appears. This will take about 2 hours.
  4. Put the raspberries with the released juice on a slight heat. Let the sugar dissolve completely.
  5. Then turn the heat up to medium and bring to a boil. With a low boil, cook for 4-5 minutes. After 5 minutes, remove from the stove, and skim off the foam.
  6. Add the remaining sugar to the hot jam cleared of foam, gently stirring, let it dissolve completely. Leave the jam to cool completely.
  7. Then repeat the boiling procedure twice, excluding the addition of sugar.
  8. After the third boiling, pour the finished peeled jam hot into prepared jars. Banks must be warm, dry, sterile. Close with lids.
