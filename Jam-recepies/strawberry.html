Strawberry jam

Description:

Recipe for strawberry jam with whole berries. Berries in strawberry-sugar syrup keep plump and not boiled.

Ingridients:

  • 1 kg - strawberries
  • 600 g - sugar

Cooking:

  1. Sprinkle strawberries with sugar and put on a slow fire.
    After about 15 minutes, the berries will release juice.
  2. Cook the jam for about 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Pour the prepared jam into jars.
    When hot, the jam will be watery, but after cooling it will thicken.
